About Us Who we are.

We are an interdisciplinary team led by Teacher Zhu Qiang, dedicated to solving computer vision related content and creating unlimited possibilities

MegaMind Lab is an inter school team led by Zhu Qiang, mainly covering Zhejiang University and Beijing Information Science&Technology University.

Here, Teacher Zhu Qiang will lead us to explore the magical mysteries contained in the field of artificial intelligence, closely integrating the human brain and machines, constantly brainstorming, and creating a new visual feast.

The era of artificial intelligence has come, but it should not be human beings that are controlled. Human wisdom is endless. When your wisdom is smart enough, robots will no longer be robots. What we need to do is to enable artificial intelligence to help humans solve problems, rather than becoming human problems. Although it is mechanical intelligence, it is humble and efficient.

Cross modal learning, human-computer interaction, data analysis, our technology is everything we need; Education, healthcare, time and space, our research encompasses everything. For the Information warfare of all mankind, do our best to create what we want.
